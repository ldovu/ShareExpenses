About...

This is a single-page web application for tracking the expenses in small group of person (as friends and family). The app allows you to:

  • Filter, search, modify and delete your expenses
  • Add an expense and split the cost equally or differently among the users
  • Check your personal balance and the balance towards a specific user
  • View your personal information in the personal section

Enjoy the app!
